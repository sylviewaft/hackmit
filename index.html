About Equip and Eat

What?

Equip and Eat provides tailored advice to individuals with medical conditions and allergies. Based on your condition and the cuisine of food you will be eating, Equip and Eat recomends key questions to ask your server, and hidden allergens to watch out for!


Why?

When certain foods can cause life threating reactions, it can be extremely scary to eat out at resturants. Equip and Eat aims to educate individuals to give them more confidence to eat out. We created Equip and Eat especially for parents of allergic children and individuals who are new to avoiding certain foods.
